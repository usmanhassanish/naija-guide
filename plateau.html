PLATEAU STATE

Plateau State is the twelfth-largest Nigerian state. It is positioned at centre of Nigeria and has a lot of hills surrounding its capital city jos, and the entire plateau itself.

Plateau State is described as "The Home of Peace and Tourism". With natural formations of rocks, hills and waterfalls, it derives its name from the Jos Plateau and has a population of around 3.5 million people.

MAJOR ECONOMIC ACTIVITIES

MINING - According to Britannica, Plateau State is considered the most significant mining area in Nigeria and also the a major exporter of tin and columbite. they also produces other mineral resources such as tantalite, kaolin, wolfram, lead, zinc and others

AGRICULTURE - Agriculture is a major occupution in plateau state. They produces cash crops such as yams, sorghum, corn, potatoes, cowpeas, rice, fruits, and vegetables
